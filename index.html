About Game Tech Library

The Game Tech Library is an in-depth library focused on helping Game Developers, Content Producers, Film Studios, Hobbyists and all levels of creatives in the real time space with one main pain point: Visual Effects and Technical Assets. This library has been built out of necessity as the real time space has rapidly expanded over the past decade one of the growing pains has been a distinct lack of visual effects artists.
